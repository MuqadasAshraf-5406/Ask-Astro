A heavenly object

Kfir Simon, taken from Tivoli Farm, Namibia

The Angel Nebula (NGC 2170) in Monoceros is a vivid skyscape with dark veins of dust overlapping regions of glowing hydrogen emission and reflected light from young stars. This HαLRGB image comprises 5.5 hours of exposure with a 24-inch scope.
